| Why High Tatras ?

High Tatras an underrated Hiking GEM of Slovakia

The mountains that you've likely never heard of. They are a part of the northern edge of Slovakia and southern border of Poland in Europe. They are the most elevated section of the Tatra chain within the Carpathian Mountains and form a natural border between the two countries. The area is popular with outdoor enthusiasts. Depending on the season, people come and visit for skiing, climbing and hiking.
